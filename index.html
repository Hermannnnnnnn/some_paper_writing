About Me

Before I started my career as a highly focused, ambitious data engineer, I didn't work that much at all 😂. After having had some traumatic experiences in my childhood, most of my growing up meant struggling to find a way forward in life. Living with trauma is like carrying extra weight around: you won't get that far living like so.

So, I ploughed and sighed and worked searching for my way: I traveled a lot, studied chemistry, mathematics, philosophy, psychology, had a long moody musical phase, was Arnold Swarchenegger at some point, was Michael Phelps at another point, some politics in the mix, and finally finished my search with spirituality in my last year of mathematics. In spirituality and mindfulness I found a way to lay down the baggage and clutter that was holding me back 😌.

After peace found its place, a new chapter arose: I found a girl, started a family and committed to IT.
